Who Are We?

  • About Us

A small charity has a big impact

The Global Nutrition Community is a vibrant network of nutrition professionals, students, and enthusiasts dedicated to promoting optimal health and well-being through evidence-based nutrition practices.

Founded in November 2023 by Rhoda Olorunseyi, the goal of the community is To build a global network of responsible and respected nutrition leaders, providing access to resources, tools, and support for our members to excel in their careers and make a positive impact in their communities.

We believe that nutrition is the foundation of health, yet often overlooked.

People frequently seek medical attention without considering the impact of their dietary habits. Meanwhile, nutritionists and dietitians face limited recognition and support. Students in the field often lack guidance on applying their knowledge in real-world scenarios. The Global Nutrition Community was established to address these gaps, to build a world where nutrition professionals are valued and recognized for their expertise, and individuals prioritize informed dietary choices for optimal health and well-being.
