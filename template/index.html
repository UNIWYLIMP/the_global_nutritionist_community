The Global Nutrition Community is a vibrant network of nutrition professionals, students, and enthusiasts dedicated to promoting optimal health and well-being through evidence-based nutrition practices. Founded in November 2023 by Rhoda Olorunseyi.
Our Mission
To build a global network of responsible and respected nutrition leaders, providing access to resources, tools, and support.
Our Vision
A world where nutrition professionals are valued and recognized for their expertise, and individuals prioritize informed dietary choices for optimal health and well-being.
